Are You Online Ready?

Colleges and universities across the country are adding online classes to the curriculum. However, online classes require a different set of skills in order to be successful than the traditional classes to which many students are accustomed. The goal of the online readiness assessment and the online readiness course is to ensure that students are properly prepared to be successful when taking online courses.

Online Readiness Assessment

The online readiness assessment is a short quiz that will help you determine whether or not you have the skills to be successful in online courses.

Online Readiness Course

Once you've taken the online readiness assessment, you may want to take the online readiness course in order to improve your skills. This six-module mini-course walks you through some of the basics that you will need to know in order to be successful online.

Objectives

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

  • Explain the benefits of online classes.
  • Log in to, navigate, and submit assignments to the Blackboard learning management system.
  • Demonstrate good, online communication skills utilizing best practices in reading, writing, and digital citizenship.
  • Prioritize time properly using best practices.
  • Create, maintain, and stick to a weekly schedule.
